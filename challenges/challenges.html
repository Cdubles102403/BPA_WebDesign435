Eco-Friendly housing


Challenges


  • Money - keeping the cost of houses down to make them available to the average consumer.
  • Suppliers - getting suppliers that produce products that meet efficiency standards and price range.
  • Planning - arguably the hardest part of the process is creating plans for a house that is easy enough to follow that the home can be created fast and efficiently.
  • Materials - Getting materials that are made from sustainable resources.
  • Ethics- getting materials from company's that support fair trade and ethical working environments.
